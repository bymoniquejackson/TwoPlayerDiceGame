A little dice game for two!
Choose which one of you would like to be player one. This player will go first. The "current" box will show your current score as the dice has been rolled.
Once a certain number is reached, player one may choose to keep their score. This will then appear in the middle of player ones section as their total score.
Player one will no longer be able to roll. Now it is player two's turn to roll. Same rules apply. If a player hits the number one withought holding any score,
the current score will reset. Once once player reaches 20 or more, they win. Have fun!
Click 'New Game' to commence!
